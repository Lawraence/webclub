Spanish Festivals

Day of the Dead

  • Date: November 1st - 2nd
  • Festive Food: Pan de muertos (sweet and fluffy bread sprinkled with sugar and has bone-shaped decorations)

Constitution Day

  • Date: December 6th
  • Festive Food: "Cocktail" (drink that looks like cocktail)
