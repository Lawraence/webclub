Introduction:

Shibei Chinese Literature Club is dedicated to the development of Chinese literary creation.Students and teachers are encouraged to participate and enjoy literary creation through our magazine published every year. Shibei literature club wishes to increase awareness of literary education of students in remote areas through the charity sale at the end of the school year.The students will also carry out various activities throughout the school year to promote Chinese culture.

拾贝文学社致力于宣传和发展汉语文学创作，希望通过每年出版的文学刊物鼓勋师生参与创作，享受创作。社团还希望借助学年末的义卖活动来资助和促进偏远地区学生的文学教育。在新的一年里，我们渴望与更多贝赛思学子一同开展活动，宣传中华文化。具有上下文菜单
