6th Grade Comp Review Links
In this page, you will find lins for the 8th grade English comprehensive exam. The links are provided by 8th grade English teachers. The links act as a quick review for students who want to check their understanding of various topics. Some links can only be functional via a VPN.
Grammar Monster
Grammer Monster provides a series of classes to each branch of the English grammar rules. These include fun videos in youtube or lessons on the Grammar Website page.
Grammar | Youtube, Grammar Monster | VPN, no VPN
Quizlet
Ms. Cobb posts flashcards for review in her class of quizlet. The flashcards act as questions in the front, and answers in the back.
Grammar | Quizlet | no VPN
